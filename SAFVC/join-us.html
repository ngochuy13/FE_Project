Join Us

Play your part in National Defence

The SAFVC welcomes applications from the wider Singapore community, namely women, first-generation Permanent Residents and new citizens between 18 and 45 years of age. Please note that Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents who have/had NS commitments are not eligible for the SAFVC.

How to apply?

SAFVC applications are open.

Click here to download SAFVC Application Form

(Please save the form on your computer first so the editable PDF will work properly).
Please send the completed form to safvc@defence.gov.sg

To schedule or change your interview appointment, please call us at 6559 0123.
(Office Hours: 8am to 5pm, Monday - Friday)
