Roles

Explore the range of roles available and find your place in SAFVC Let's find the perfect role for you

To enable SAFVC Volunteers to serve meaningfully, the SAFVC has introduced a variety of roles in nine categories across three services: Army, Air Force and Navy. These roles range from security and naval operations, to specialised fields such as psychology and nursing. SAFVC Volunteers will be assigned to specific roles based on their medical fitness and overall assessed aptitude.

Security

The role of an Auxiliary Security Trooper is to augment security operations in support of Singapore Armed Forces (SAF) events, functions and other SAF–organised activities (e.g. National Day Parade, Open Houses) as well as to assist with security operations at access control points of civilian key installations (e.g. Airport) or SAF camps/bases.

Auxiliary Security Trooper
Naval Operations

There are two roles available under the Naval Operations category - Bridge Watchkeeper and Deck Operator (Seamanship).

Bridge Watchkeeper
Deck Operator (Seamanship)
Information

The role of an InfoMedia Staff is to support the SAF public communications efforts such as media management and event publicity.

InfoMedia Staff
Command, Control, Communications And Computers (C4)

Command, Control, Communications and Computers (C4) Experts provide the Republic of Singapore Air Force (RSAF) with ‘niche’ expertise in the areas of Satellite Communications and Radio Communications and Networks. The role of a C4 Expert is to support the planning of the use of radio communications, electromagnetic spectrum management and satellite communications. They are also required to provide updates and recommendations on the best commercial practices and technologies to the Republic of Singapore Air Force (RSAF).

C4 Expert
Engineering

There are three roles available under the Engineering category – Airbase Civil Engineer, Naval Safety Engineer and Naval Combat/Platform Engineer.

Airbase Civil Engineer Naval Combat / Platform Engineer
Naval Safety Engineer
Legal

The role of a Legal Specialist Staff is to provide legal support during operations and to share best practices.

Legal Specialist Staff
Maritime Trainers

There are two roles available under the Maritime Trainers category – Merchant Ship Engineering Trainer and Maritime Ship Operations Trainer.

Merchant Ship Engineering Trainer
Merchant Ship Operations Trainer
Medical Trainers

There are five roles available under the Medical Trainers category – Doctor, Nurse, Dentist, Radiographer and Medical Technologist.

Doctor Dentist Medical Technologist
Nurse Radiographer
Defence Psychologist

The role of a Defence Psychologist is to assist in the development and implementation of Psychological Defence capabilities in the Singapore Armed Forces (SAF), and provide comprehensive psychology support along a peace to war continuum.

Defence Psychologist
