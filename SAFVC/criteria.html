Criteria

We welcome applications from the wider Singapore Community

Women, first-generation Permanent Residents and new citizens between 18 and 45 years of age are welcome to apply. Applicants will undergo interview and medical screening to assess if they meet the necessary medical status and overall aptitude to undergo training and deployment.

Citizenship Status

Singapore Citizen Singapore Permanent Resident

Gender

Males and Females

Age

18 - 45 years old (service period is up to 50 years old)

*Please note that Singaporeans or Singapore Permanent Residents who have/had NS commitments and wish to extend their voluntary service to the SAFVC may apply for the Reservist on Voluntary Extended Reserve Service (ROVERS) Scheme instead.
